# seo recovery plan 

## Project
SEO recovery brief for **kingdomdesignhouse.com**

## Purpose
This file captures the current SEO diagnosis and should be used as the source context for generating a prioritized recovery plan to improve rankings, CTR, page relevance, and local search visibility.

---

## 1. Current Diagnosis

Main findings:

- Rankings are weak across most service pages.
- Impressions are spread across too many service categories.
- The homepage performs best.
- Pricing is likely not the primary issue.
- The biggest problem is diluted topical focus.

Expanded summary:

The site is receiving impressions across web design, web development, AI, and network-related searches, but most service pages rank too low to generate meaningful clicks. The homepage is the strongest performer, which suggests Google has more confidence in the overall brand entry point than in the deeper service pages. The data does not currently support pricing as the main cause of underperformance. The stronger explanation is that the site is trying to rank for too many service categories at once, which weakens topical authority and page relevance.

---

## 2. Best-Performing Pages

These pages currently show the strongest traction and should be treated as high-priority assets:

- Homepage
- About page
- AI consulting page
- Contact page

Notes:

- The homepage appears to be the strongest organic asset.
- The About page is performing better than many service pages, which may indicate branded or trust-driven engagement.
- The AI consulting page has some traction, but ranking strength is still limited.
- The Contact page receives some visibility, suggesting a portion of users are moving toward direct brand/contact intent.

---

## 3. Weak but Visible Pages

These pages have impressions but weak rankings and low click performance:

- `/web-group/`
- `/web-group/services/web-development/`
- `/web-group/services/support/`
- `/network-group/services/network-design/`
- `/network-group/services/network-support/`
- `/ai-group/services/ai-support/`
- `/ai-group/services/ai-development/`

Notes:

- These pages are being indexed and shown in search.
- Most are not ranking high enough to earn meaningful clicks.
- These pages should be reviewed first for page intent, keyword alignment, title tags, headings, internal links, content depth, and location/service specificity.

---

## 4. Keyword Pattern Summary

The site is currently appearing for mixed search intent across these categories:

- Web design
- Web development
- AI consulting
- AI support
- Network support
- Wireless/network infrastructure
- Multiple locations

Interpretation:

This pattern suggests the site lacks a tight SEO focus. Instead of building strong authority in one primary service lane, impressions are fragmented across several unrelated or loosely related commercial intents. This likely reduces Google's confidence in the site as a top result for any one category.

---

## 5. Strategic Conclusion

The site should prioritize one primary SEO lane instead of splitting authority across web, AI, and network services equally.
